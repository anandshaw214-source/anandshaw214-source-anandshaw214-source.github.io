Marketing Principles
This course introduces students to the fundamental concepts, issues, and challenges faced by managers responsible for marketing in for-profit organizations.  Students learn new concepts, conduct extensive library research, and develop a marketing strategy for a new product or service they have designed.  

Marketing Research
This course is offered mostly to marketing and communication majors.  Students conduct extensive secondary and primary research, learn to conceptualize new studies, design measurement instruments, collect and analyze data.

Marketing Strategy & Concepts
This is an advanced course offered in the MBA program; it examines marketing as a strategic choice of the firm.  The course is Harvard Business School Case Study - based.  Students conduct extensive analysis, and present their reasoned arguments in front of the class [every other week].
